Ganivada Sree Prasanna Durga Gayatri is the founding Principal Advocate of Ganivada & Associates, a boutique law chambers in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. She practises at High Courts, Trial Courts, and Tribunals across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, with a focus on civil, criminal, arbitration, property, and commercial legal matters.

She holds a BA LLB (Hons.) from Ramaiah Institute of Legal Studies, Bengaluru, graduating with Grade A in 2023. During her studies, she completed internships at some of India's and the world's most respected legal institutions: including Clifford Chance (ranked globally in the top 10 by revenue), the chambers of the Advocate General of Karnataka at the High Court, and the Supreme Court chambers of Advocate Pradeep Rai, Vice President of the Supreme Court Bar Association.

Her professional career began at Venkat & Venkat Advocates & Solicitors, Hyderabad, where she spent over a year in full-time practice across criminal, civil, property, and international commercial law matters. She also works as a Legal Freelancer, providing contract drafting and advisory services to domestic and international clients.

Advocate Ganivada Sree Prasanna Durga Gayatri brings to every matter a combination of rigorous legal analysis, exposure to diverse practice areas, and a commitment to client-focused outcomes.
