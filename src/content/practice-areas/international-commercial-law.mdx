## International Commercial Law Practice

We advise foreign companies, individuals, and international law firms on India-related commercial and legal matters.

## How We Help

- **Cross-Border Contracts**: Drafting and reviewing contracts involving foreign parties, including distribution agreements, service agreements, technology licences, and supply contracts.
- **Inbound Investment Advisory**: Advising on the legal framework for foreign direct investment in India, including FDI regulations, corporate structuring, and sectoral restrictions.
- **International Arbitration**: Representing parties in international commercial arbitrations with Indian parties or India-seated proceedings.
- **Enforcement of Foreign Judgments and Awards**: Advising on enforcing foreign court judgments and arbitral awards in India.
- **Foreign Client Services**: Providing India-specific legal advice to foreign individuals and businesses, including NRIs and foreign nationals owning property in India.
- **Data Protection and Privacy**: Advising on India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 compliance for companies operating in or with India.

## Typical Matters

- Cross-border service and technology agreements with Indian counterparties
- International arbitrations involving Indian parties
- NRI property matters in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana
- Advising foreign companies on Indian contract law and dispute resolution
- Enforcement and challenge of foreign arbitral awards in India
