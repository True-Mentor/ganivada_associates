## Real Estate Law Practice

We advise buyers, sellers, developers, landlords, and tenants on real estate transactions and disputes in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

## How We Help

- **Title Verification**: Due diligence on property title, encumbrances, and encroachments before purchase.
- **Transaction Documentation**: Drafting and reviewing sale deeds, agreements to sell, general powers of attorney, mortgage deeds, and lease agreements.
- **RERA Advisory**: Advising buyers and developers on RERA registration, project compliance, and complaints before the AP RERA Authority.
- **Real Estate Disputes**: Representing parties in property disputes including specific performance suits, possession suits, and injunctions.
- **Builder-Buyer Disputes**: Advising homebuyers in disputes with developers regarding delayed possession, defects, and cancellations.
- **Rental and Tenancy**: Advising landlords and tenants on leave and licence agreements, eviction proceedings, and rent disputes.

## Typical Matters

- Pre-purchase title due diligence in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana
- Sale deed and agreement drafting for residential and commercial property
- RERA complaints by homebuyers
- Lease and tenancy disputes
- Joint development agreements and builder agreements
- Encroachment and partition suits relating to real property
