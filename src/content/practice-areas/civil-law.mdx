## Civil Law Practice

Our chambers advises and represents clients in a wide range of civil proceedings before District Courts, Civil Courts, and High Courts in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

## How We Help

- **Money Recovery Suits**: Drafting plaints and representing clients in suits for recovery of money, including suits on promissory notes and agreements.
- **Injunctions**: Seeking and defending temporary and permanent injunctions to protect rights and prevent irreparable harm.
- **Specific Performance**: Enforcement of contractual obligations, particularly in property and commercial agreements.
- **Declaratory Suits**: Obtaining declarations of rights, titles, and legal status before competent courts.
- **Civil Appeals & Revisions**: Appealing civil decrees and orders before the High Courts and appellate civil courts.
- **Interlocutory Applications**: Drafting and arguing IAs for interim relief in pending suits.

## Typical Matters

- Money recovery and debt enforcement proceedings
- Property-related civil suits (title, possession, partition)
- Enforcement of agreements and contracts
- Injunction proceedings in commercial and personal disputes
- Civil appeals from decrees of subordinate courts
- Writ petitions relating to civil rights before the High Court
