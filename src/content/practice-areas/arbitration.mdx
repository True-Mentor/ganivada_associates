## Arbitration & ADR Practice

Our chambers advises and represents clients in domestic and international arbitration proceedings, as well as in mediation and conciliation under Indian and international frameworks.

## How We Help

- **Domestic Arbitration**: Representing parties in ad hoc and institutional arbitrations under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.
- **International Arbitration**: Advising on arbitral proceedings involving foreign parties or cross-border contracts, including jurisdictional and enforcement questions.
- **Drafting Arbitration Clauses**: Designing effective dispute resolution clauses in commercial contracts to minimise litigation risk.
- **Enforcement of Awards**: Enforcing domestic and foreign arbitral awards before Indian courts, and opposing enforcement where grounds exist.
- **Mediation & Conciliation**: Representing clients in court-annexed and private mediation proceedings.
- **Injunctions in Aid of Arbitration**: Seeking interim relief under Section 9 of the Arbitration Act from courts.

## Typical Matters

- Commercial contract disputes referred to arbitration
- Construction and infrastructure arbitrations
- International trade and supply-chain disputes
- Real estate and joint venture arbitrations
- Enforcement or challenge of arbitral awards
- Conciliation under the Mediation Act, 2023
