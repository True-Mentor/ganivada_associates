## Family Law Practice

We advise and represent clients in matrimonial, custody, maintenance, and succession matters before Family Courts and civil courts in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

## How We Help

- **Divorce Proceedings**: Mutual consent and contested divorce under Hindu, Muslim, Christian, and Special Marriage Acts.
- **Child Custody and Guardianship**: Interim and final custody applications, visitation rights, and guardianship proceedings.
- **Maintenance**: Applications for interim and permanent maintenance for spouses and children.
- **Domestic Violence**: Representing applicants in Protection Order, Residence Order, and Monetary Relief applications under the DV Act.
- **Matrimonial Property**: Advising on the division of matrimonial property upon separation or divorce.
- **Succession and Inheritance**: Advising on succession under the Hindu Succession Act, Indian Succession Act, and drafting of Wills.

## Typical Matters

- Divorce petitions under the Hindu Marriage Act and Special Marriage Act
- Custody and guardianship applications in Family Courts
- Interim and permanent maintenance proceedings
- Protection orders under the Domestic Violence Act
- Succession certificates and letters of administration
- Wills and estate planning for individual clients
