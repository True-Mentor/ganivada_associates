## Document Drafting Practice

Precise, well-researched legal drafting is central to every matter we handle. We provide standalone drafting services for clients who require specific legal documents.

## How We Help

- **Legal Notices**: Notices for breach of contract, recovery of money, property disputes, service matters, and defamation.
- **Plaints**: Drafting of plaints for civil suits: money recovery, injunction, specific performance, and declaratory suits.
- **Written Statements**: Comprehensive written statements for defendants in civil proceedings, including detailed admissions, denials, and affirmative defences.
- **Interlocutory Applications (IAs)**: Applications for temporary injunctions, stay orders, receivers, and other interim relief.
- **Civil Revision Petitions**: Petitions under Section 115 CPC challenging interlocutory orders in civil proceedings.
- **Affidavits and Sworn Statements**: Affidavits-in-evidence, affidavits of service, and supporting affidavits for court proceedings.
- **Writ Petitions**: Drafting writ petitions under Articles 226 and 227 of the Constitution for High Court filings.

## Typical Matters

- Legal notices in contract, property, and employment disputes
- Plaints for civil suits initiating proceedings in subordinate courts
- IAs for temporary injunctions in pending civil suits
- Revision petitions from interlocutory orders of civil courts
- Affidavits and other supporting court documents
- Writ petitions before the High Courts of AP and Telangana
