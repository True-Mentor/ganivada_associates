## Property Law Practice

We advise on and litigate property rights, ownership disputes, and related matters in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

## How We Help

- **Title Disputes**: Representing parties in suits for declaration of title and injunctions against interference with possession.
- **Partition Suits**: Representing co-owners in suits for partition and separate possession of jointly held property.
- **Adverse Possession**: Advising on claims and defences relating to adverse possession of land.
- **Property Documentation**: Drafting and reviewing gift deeds, settlement deeds, partition deeds, and relinquishment deeds.
- **Encroachment Matters**: Seeking and opposing injunctions in cases of alleged encroachment on private property.
- **Revenue Record Corrections**: Advising on rectification of entries in revenue records (pattadar passbooks, ROR) and related proceedings.

## Typical Matters

- Family property partition among legal heirs and co-owners
- Title disputes between neighbours and adjacent landowners
- Encroachment and boundary disputes
- Gift deed, settlement deed, and succession-related documentation
- Revenue record entries and correction of pattadar records
- Adverse possession claims and defences
