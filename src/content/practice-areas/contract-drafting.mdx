## Contract Drafting Practice

We draft, review, and negotiate a wide range of commercial contracts for individuals, businesses, and international clients.

## How We Help

- **Commercial Agreements**: Service agreements, vendor contracts, consultancy agreements, and distribution arrangements.
- **Employment Documentation**: Employment contracts, contractor agreements, HR policies, and non-compete clauses.
- **NDAs and Confidentiality**: Non-disclosure agreements for business negotiations, joint ventures, and technology sharing.
- **MoUs and Term Sheets**: Memoranda of Understanding, letters of intent, and term sheets for proposed transactions.
- **Shareholder Agreements**: Investor rights, drag-along and tag-along provisions, anti-dilution, and exit mechanisms.
- **Contract Reviews**: Detailed review of third-party contracts with identified risks and suggested amendments.
- **International Contracts**: Cross-border agreements with choice of law, jurisdiction, and international arbitration clauses.

## Typical Matters

- Service agreements for software, consulting, and professional services businesses
- Supply and vendor contracts for manufacturing and trading companies
- International contracts with foreign counterparties
- Shareholder and founders agreements for startups and SMEs
- Franchise and licensing agreements
- Pre-nuptial and family settlement agreements
