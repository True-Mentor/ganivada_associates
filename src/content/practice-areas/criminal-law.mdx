## Criminal Law Practice

We represent individuals and entities in criminal proceedings across Sessions Courts, Magistrate Courts, and High Courts in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

## How We Help

- **Bail Applications**: Regular bail, anticipatory bail, and bail on merits before Magistrate Courts, Sessions Courts, and High Courts.
- **Defence Representation**: Full representation of accused persons through trial, including cross-examination of witnesses and final arguments.
- **Criminal Appeals**: Appeals against convictions, sentences, and acquittals before Sessions Courts and High Courts.
- **Quashing Petitions**: Petitions under Section 482 CrPC (BNSS) for quashing of FIRs and criminal proceedings before the High Court.
- **Complaint Proceedings**: Representing complainants in private complaints and compoundable offences.
- **Bail Cancellation Matters**: Opposing bail applications and arguing for bail cancellation where clients are victims or complainants.

## Typical Matters

- Bail applications in IPC / BNS, economic offences, and special legislation cases
- Criminal trials in Sessions and Magistrate Courts
- Appeals against conviction or acquittal
- PMLA and economic offences (in conjunction with our Financial Law practice)
- Domestic violence matters and protection orders
- Criminal cases arising from property and commercial disputes
